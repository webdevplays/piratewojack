1. Create a wallet
Download Phantom Wallet or another SOL wallet of your choice from the App Store or Google Play Store for free. Desktop users can also install the Phantom Chrome extension.
2. Get some $SOL
Have SOL in your wallet to swap into PIRATEWOJAK. If you don’t already have SOL, you can buy it from an exchange and transfer it to your wallet.
3. Go to Raydium
Go to Raydium (via the web app or mobile browser) and connect your wallet. Make sure your SOL is visible in the wallet.
4. Swap
Swap SOL for PIRATEWOJAK and confirm the transaction. The tokens will appear in your wallet once the transaction is complete.
